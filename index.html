FAQ

Questions answered.

Everything you need to know before starting. Still unsure? Book a free call — no pitch, just answers.

What does "Figma to code" actually mean?
We design your entire site in Figma first — you see and approve every screen before a single line of code is written. Then we hand-code it into a production-ready website.
How long does a project take?
Starter projects take 1–2 weeks. Growth projects run 3–5 weeks. Premium is 6–10 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before we start.
Do I own the design files?
Yes — 100%. After completion you receive all Figma source files, code repository access, and full deployment credentials. Everything is yours.
What if I need changes after launch?
Growth and Premium packages include post-launch support. Starter clients can purchase a support retainer. We also offer ongoing monthly maintenance plans.
Which tech stack do you use?
Primarily React and Next.js. We also build in HTML/CSS/JS and can integrate with Webflow, Framer, or your preferred CMS.
How do we get started?
Book a free 30-minute discovery call. We'll understand your goals, scope the project, and send a proposal within 48 hours.
