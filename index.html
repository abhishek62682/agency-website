FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before starting. Still unsure? Schedule a free call — no sales, just answers.

What does "Figma to code" mean?
We design your website in Figma first, letting you approve every screen. Then we convert it into a fully coded, production-ready site.
How long does a project take?
Starter projects usually take 1–2 weeks. Growth projects take 3–5 weeks. Premium projects are 6–10 weeks. Timelines are shared before we start.
Do I own the design files?
Yes. You receive all Figma files, the full code repository, and deployment credentials. Everything belongs to you.
Can I request changes after launch?
Starter clients can request a few minor changes after launch if needed. Growth and Premium packages include full post-launch support, and ongoing monthly maintenance is available.
Which tech stack do you use?
We primarily use React and Next.js for the frontend, Node.js with Express for the backend, and MongoDB for the database.
How do we get started?
Schedule a free 30-minute discovery call. We'll understand your goals, scope your project, and send a proposal within 48 hours.
